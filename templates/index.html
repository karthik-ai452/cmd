How it works

Four steps. Zero code.

1

Upload your test document

Take the test case document your QA team already maintains — a Word doc, an Excel sheet, or a PDF — and upload it to CmdRunner. No special formatting. No test scripting language. Just the natural-language steps your team already writes.

2

Agentic AI executes every step

Instead of only following predefined selectors or scripts, CmdRunner's AI understands the intent behind the test steps and adapts dynamically while interacting with the application. It navigates, performs actions, and validates results — interacting with your software like a real human user.

3

Human in the loop

CmdRunner pauses at key checkpoints — assertions, sensitive steps, or ambiguous outcomes — and routes them to a human reviewer for approval. Your team stays in control of critical decisions, with full context from screenshots and execution logs at their fingertips. Approve, request changes, or add notes — all before a test is marked complete.

4

Get validated results with full evidence

CmdRunner validates the results against your expected outcomes and generates execution reports automatically — step-by-step screenshots, action logs, and a clear pass/fail verdict tied back to the original test case. Your team reviews the results, not the execution.
