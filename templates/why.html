What it does

Upload a document. Get fully executed test results.

Instead of relying heavily on scripting, brittle selectors, or complex automation frameworks, users can upload test cases from documents such as Excel sheets — and CmdRunner understands the intent of each step, navigates the application, performs actions, validates results, and generates execution reports automatically.

01

Upload your test document

Drop in a Word, Excel, or PDF file containing your test cases. CmdRunner supports the formats your team already uses — no templates, special formatting, or scripting required.

02

AI understands the intent of each step

CmdRunner's Agentic AI parses natural-language instructions and understands what each step is trying to achieve — not just which selector to click, but why. It identifies actions, locators, and expected outcomes from plain English.

03

Interacts like a real human user

The agent opens a live browser session and interacts with the application the way a human QA engineer would — reading labels, clicking buttons, filling forms, and navigating multi-step workflows across your web application.

Instead of only following predefined selectors or scripts, the AI understands the intent behind the test steps and adapts dynamically while interacting with the application — so your tests keep working even as your UI evolves.

Every execution produces screenshots, step-by-step logs, and a pass/fail report your team can review. Full traceability from the original document step to the final result — automatically.
